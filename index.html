Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury timepieces, ordering process, and customer care

Every MIDEX GOLD watch comes with a certificate of authenticity, unique serial number, and official documentation. You can verify your timepiece through our online authentication system using the serial number located on the case back. We also recommend purchasing directly from our authorized retailers or official website to guarantee authenticity.

All MIDEX GOLD timepieces come with a 5-year international warranty that covers manufacturing defects and mechanical issues. The warranty includes:

  • Full movement service and repairs
  • Replacement of defective components
  • Water resistance testing and resealing
  • Timekeeping accuracy adjustments

The warranty does not cover damage caused by accidents, improper handling, unauthorized repairs, or normal wear and tear of components like straps and crystals.

We offer several shipping options to ensure your timepiece arrives safely and promptly:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days - Fully insured
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days - Priority handling with full insurance
  • White Glove Delivery: By appointment - Personal delivery, setup, and explanation of your timepiece (available in select metropolitan areas)

All shipments include insurance, tracking, and require signature confirmation. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on customs processing.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn watches with all original packaging and documents intact. For returns:

  • Items must be in original condition with protective films intact
  • All certificates and documents must be included
  • Return shipping is complimentary within the first 14 days
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days of receiving the returned item

Exchanges are available within 60 days of purchase. Custom and engraved timepieces are final sale unless defective.

Proper maintenance ensures your timepiece remains accurate and beautiful for generations:

  • Daily Care: Wipe with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid contact with chemicals, solvents, and salt water
  • Water Resistance: Have water resistance checked annually if frequently exposed to water
  • Service Intervals: Mechanical watches should be serviced every 3-5 years
  • Magnetic Fields: Avoid prolonged exposure to strong magnetic fields
  • Storage: Store in original box away from extreme temperatures and humidity

We offer comprehensive service packages starting at $250, including complete movement overhaul, cleaning, and pressure testing.

Yes, we offer several customization options to make your timepiece uniquely yours:

  • Strap Options: Choose from alligator leather, stainless steel bracelets, rubber straps, or NATO bands in various colors
  • Personal Engraving: Add initials, special dates, or short messages on case backs (maximum 20 characters)
  • Custom Dials: Select from exclusive dial colors and finishes (available on certain models)
  • Buckle Options: Upgrade to deployment clasps or custom-designed buckles

Customization options may extend delivery time by 2-3 weeks. All customizations are final and cannot be returned unless defective.

We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. International orders are subject to the following:

  • All applicable customs duties, taxes, and import fees are the responsibility of the recipient
  • We complete all necessary customs documentation accurately
  • Shipping costs include insurance up to the full retail value
  • Delivery times vary by destination (typically 7-21 business days)
  • Some countries may have restrictions on luxury goods imports

We recommend checking with your local customs office for specific import regulations and potential fees. For orders over $5,000, we recommend using our premium international shipping service which includes customs clearance assistance.
